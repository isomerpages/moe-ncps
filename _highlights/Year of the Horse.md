<h4>Nan Chiau Celebrates the Year of the Horse</h4>

Nan Chiau Primary School celebrated Lunar New Year on 16 February. This year’s programme featured a rich variety of performances, including guzheng, festive drumming, wushu, and lion dance. In addition, our teachers thoughtfully prepared a Chinese New Year musical skit titled 《小马智斗年兽》Students also took part in a series of classroom and recess activities, such as Little Horse handicrafts, paper cutting, Fú (福) texture rubbing and lucky charm keychain crafting.
