Our school’s Hari Raya celebration was a lively and enriching experience for everyone. The concert opened with a skit, a Malay dance, and an interactive quiz by Wira Budaya.  Students showcased their talents through a silat performance and the singing of a Hari Raya song, while teachers joined in with a cheerful Malay dance. A fashion show featuring both teachers and students also highlighted the beauty of traditional Malay attire. 
During recess, students enjoyed hands-on activities such as kuih making, ketupat weaving, and creating ‘green packets’, allowing them to connect with the traditions in a fun and memorable way. <br>
