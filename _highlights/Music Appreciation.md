<h4>Singapore Soundscape</h4>

![](/images/MAP_2026__websize__1.jpg)
The Music Committee invited Pasat Merdu to perform at the first assembly of 2026. This performance explored how music travelled across borders, shaping identity and culture. Pupils had the opportunity to watch live performances featuring traditional instruments such as the pipa, rebana,&nbsp;oud, tabla and violin, representing the Chinese, Malay and Indian communities in Singapore.

Students clapped along enthusiastically and thoroughly enjoyed the performance, demonstrating their appreciation for Singapore’s rich multicultural soundscape.
