<h4>School Anniversary Celebration – I Love Nan Chiau “我爱南侨”</h4>

Nan Chiau’s anniversary celebration brought together four generations of the school community.

The Nan Chiau Alumni Association (NCAA) refurbished chairs from the auditorium of Nan Chiau High School’s Kim Yam Road campus. A highlight of the concert was the Gifting of Artefact Ceremony, where NCAA presented these chairs to the school, commemorating this historical moment. This gesture reflected that year’s theme, I Love Nan Chiau (“我爱南侨”).

The celebration concluded with the 4th Homecoming Lunch, where alumni gathered to reconnect, share memories, and reminisce about their Nan Chiau stories together.
