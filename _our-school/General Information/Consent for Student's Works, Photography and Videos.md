<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Photographs or video images of your child/ward may be taken during school
activities and events such as classroom lessons, CCA, school camps, or
school concerts. The school may use and publish such photographs or video
recordings including live streaming of school events, in school publications,the
school’s website, social media channels, Singapore Hokkien Huay Kuan (SHHK)
publications, or other communication channels.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Student’s works created during their course of study in the school may
be used for non-commercial purposes.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Opt-out process: If you do not wish for your child’s/ward’s photographs
or video images to be used, please inform the school in writing.</p>
<p></p>
</li>
</ul>
