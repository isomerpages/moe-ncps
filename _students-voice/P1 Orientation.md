On 2 January 2026, Nan Chiau Primary School warmly welcomed 300 new Primary 1 students and their families to our annual Orientation Programme. The programme featured curriculum briefings by various Heads of Department, parent-child recess sessions, and ICON onboarding activities. This comprehensive Orientation Programme eased students’ transition into primary school life and fostered a strong home-school partnership from the start.
