Our Primary 3 students enjoyed an enriching learning journey to the Singapore Zoo in Term 1. As part of their Science topic on Animal Groups, they observed diverse wildlife up close. Through guided exploration, students identified unique traits and classified animals by body coverings. This authentic, hands-on experience brought classroom learning to life and deepened their curiosity about the animal kingdom.
